About Me

After working in IT for a wholesale supplier for 5 years, I made the career move to the software supplier we had implemented. I would work there for the next 20 years. Our start-up would eventually be bought by Intuit, sold to a competitor (Activant Solutions) and finally merged with another software company (Epicor).

Along the way I transitioned from Implementation Consulting to Product Management after eight years. I was always fascinated by the process of creating software and loved being part of the creative process to make the software better for the industries it served.

What's next? I beleive that it is time to take my experience and create my own vision of what ERP software should be. Big things to come...
